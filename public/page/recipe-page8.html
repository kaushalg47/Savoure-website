The Recipe


Craving something lip-smacking but don't want to spend hours in the kitchen making a dish? Then we have the perfect dish for you. Prepared in less than 15 minutes, this yummy paneer dish can be either served as a main dish or enjoyed as a snack. Paneer is quite rich in protein, which makes this dish weight-loss friendly. We have used just 1 spoon olive oil to make it, which makes the recipe quite healthy. The best thing about this dish is that veggies don't need to be cooked for long. You just need to toss and cook the veggies to keep them crunchy. You can add green beans, carrot, cabbage and even corn to make the dish more nutritious. This Paneer Chilly recipe is prepared without adding any sauces like soy sauce, ketchup or schezwan sauce but is still equally good in taste.

Steps to preperation:


Step 1 Saute the ingredients Heat olive oil in a pan. Add asafoetida, cumin seeds, minced garlic and dry red chilli. Let them splutter for a minute. Now add cubed onions and saute them for two minutes.
Step 2 Add the veggies Now add cubed tomatoes and capsicum. Toss them and saute for another two minutes.
Step 3 Add the spices Now add salt, red chilli powder, coriander powder, dry mango powder and garam masala. Give a good mix. Cook the masala for two minutes.
Step 4 Add paneer Now add paneer cubes and toss well to coat them with the masala. Add 2-3 tbsp water to form steam and quickly toss the ingredients. Cook for 5 minutes on medium flame and keep tossing in between.
Step 5 Ready to be served Garnish the dish with chopped spring onions and serve.
